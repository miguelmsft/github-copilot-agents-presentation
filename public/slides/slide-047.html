What Are Agent Skills?

Skills are folders of instructions, scripts, and resources that Copilot loads when relevant to the current task.

Think of them as recipe cards for the agent — step-by-step procedures for specific tasks.

  • Based on an open standard (works across Copilot, Claude, and more)
  • Automatically activated by relevance, or manually invoked
  • Stored as SKILL.md files with YAML frontmatter
Skills solve the problem of specialized knowledge. Your team probably has documented procedures for things like debugging CI failures, generating release notes, or deploying to staging. Skills let you encode those procedures in a format that Copilot can follow. The agent reads the skill's description, and when it encounters a matching task, it loads the full instructions. It's like giving a new team member a runbook.
