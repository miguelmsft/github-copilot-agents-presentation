Copilot scans all three directory formats — maximum cross-tool compatibility.

Skills can be stored at the project level in your repo — shared with your team via version control — or at the personal level in your home directory, shared across all your projects. Notice that Copilot scans three different directory formats: `.github/skills/`, `.claude/skills/`, and `.agents/skills/`. This means a skill written for Claude Code works in Copilot without any changes, and vice versa. It's a truly portable format.
