Choose the right level of autonomy for each task.

Think of it as a spectrum. On the left, you're fully interactive — reviewing every suggestion. Moving right, you give the agent more autonomy. The CLI in workspace mode still asks for approvals. The CLI in worktree mode runs freely in an isolated branch. And the cloud agent works completely autonomously in the cloud, creating a PR for review. The right choice depends on the task, your trust level, and whether you need team collaboration.
