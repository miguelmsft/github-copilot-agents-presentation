The Big Shift

BEFORE: You type code → Copilot suggests next line
 AFTER: You describe a goal → Copilot plans, codes, tests, and iterates
Think of it this way. Before, Copilot was like GPS giving you turn-by-turn directions — you still had to drive. Now, it's more like a self-driving car. You set the destination, and it figures out the route, handles obstacles, and gets you there. You're still in the driver's seat — you can steer, redirect, or take over at any time — but the car does the heavy lifting. Let's understand how that works.
