Before we continue with more examples, let's step back and see these four mechanisms side by side. The key differentiators are: how they're triggered, what they can include, and how they affect context usage. Custom instructions are always loaded — great for standards but costly on context. Skills are the most efficient — only loaded when relevant. Prompt files and custom agents are on-demand. Notice that only skills support bundled scripts, and only skills use the open standard format that works across multiple AI tools.
