Rule of thumb: If it applies to every task → Custom Instructions. If it applies to specific tasks → Skill.

This is a common point of confusion. Custom instructions and skills both provide guidance to Copilot, but they serve different purposes. Custom instructions are always on — they apply to every interaction, so use them for things like coding standards. Skills are loaded on demand — use them for specific procedures like debugging CI or deploying to staging. If you put everything in custom instructions, you waste context tokens on irrelevant guidance.
