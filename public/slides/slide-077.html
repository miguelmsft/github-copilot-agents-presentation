Let's recap what we covered today. Copilot agents are autonomous AI systems that follow the Understand-Act-Validate loop. They run in four places — VS Code, terminal, cloud, and third-party providers. MCP connects them to external tools. Skills teach them specialized workflows. Hooks enforce security policies. And seven customization mechanisms let you tailor everything to your team's needs. All of these work together as complementary layers.
